Introduction to Origami


Origami is the art of paper folding, which is often associated with Japanese culture.
In modern usage, the word "origami" is used as an inclusive term for all folding practices, regardless of their culture of origin.

Intresting facts about Butterflies (CLICK THE ABOVE IMAGE TO WATCH THE STEPS TO MAKE IT)

  • 1. Butterflies range in size from a tiny 1/8 inch to a huge almost 12 inches.
  • 2. Butterflies cannot fly if their body temperature is less than 86 degrees.
  • 3. The Brimstone butterfly (Gonepterix rhamni) has the longest lifetime of the adult butterflies: 9-10 months!
  • 4. Many butterflies can taste with their feet to find out whether the leaf they sit on is good to lay eggs on to be their caterpillars’ food or not.
  • 5. They don’t have a nose or even lungs. Butterflies have tiny holes called spiracles on their outer skeleton (or exoskeleton), through which they breathe in oxygen.
